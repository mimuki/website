But why?

I keep getting asked what things I like, because buying gifts is hard. If you buy me something from here, I am 100% going to like it and use it often. You could also use this page as inspiration for your own, cooler ideas.

If you want to buy me something on this list- message me, or just send me the money on kofi with a note for what it's for. If we live together, you know where I live, and can gift accordingly. I'm also using this to keep track of stuff I'm saving for, so some of these are... more expensive than the average birthday gift, lmao.

Prices are in AUD, but may vary- check the link just in case, sometimes there are sales or surprise inflation. If you click through you're at the mercy of whatever website I've linked, but I haven't added any affiliate junk or extra tracking.
