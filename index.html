Who am I?

Good question! Some phrases that could be used to describe me are:

  • bitter cripplepunk disaster
  • novice contemplative
  • DIY cyborg
  • consistently inconsistent
  • regretful computer toucher

My pronouns are he/him, except when they aren't. I'm in my mid 20s, and am reaching that point in life where it's too much effort to increment my age on websites every year.

Why a website?

I have lots of things I feel like saying, and I make this site first for me, and second for anyone who will benefit from it. I design it based on my own whims, but ideally without totally sacrificing accessibility.

You might remember the old website- it was very pretty, but it was not making me happy. I was overwhelmed by its complexity, and I'm trying a more plaintexty approach, and taking things extra slow, even if that means things look a bit bland and boring for now.
