Who am I?

Good question! Some phrases that could be used to describe me are:

  • bitter cripplepunk disaster
  • novice contemplative
  • DIY cyborg
  • consistently inconsistent
  • regretful computer toucher

I go by whatever username(s) you know me as. My pronouns are he/him, except when they aren't. I'm in my mid 20s, and am reaching that point in life where it's too much effort to increment my age on websites every year.

Why a website?

I have lots of things I feel like saying, and writing them down in a website makes it easier to share with my friends. Sometimes new friends will get to stumble upon this stuff too. That's fun. I also think websites are neat as self expression, a last hurrah of defiance against a homogenous, corporate internet.
